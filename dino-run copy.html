How to play

Controls

Jump — press Space, the Up arrow, or click/tap the game.

Duck — hold the Down arrow to slide under low-flying birds. Hold it while airborne to drop back down faster.

Goal

Survive as long as you can. Your score climbs the further you run, and the pace quickens the longer you last. Day will flip to night every so often — purely cosmetic, the challenge stays the same.

Difficulty

Easy — slow start, wide gaps between obstacles, birds are rare.
Medium — a brisker pace with tighter gaps and more frequent birds.
Hard — fastest pace, the tightest gaps, and birds appear often.

Scoring

Each difficulty keeps its own separate high score. If you beat it mid-run, you'll get a quick notice — it won't affect your gameplay at all. Beat it for good and confetti will be waiting for you on the game over screen.

Heads up

Use the home button in the corner to return here anytime. If the game sits untouched for 5 minutes, it returns here on its own.
